Project 5...

Sustainable Bus

Category Sketch
Year 2023

This vehicle sketch focuses on designing a futuristic sustainable bus. This innovative vehicle integrates a solar panel on its roof, harnessing solar energy to power its operations. To ensure uninterrupted functionality, even on cloudy days, the bus is equipped with a backup charging system. The bus is designed to be driverless, with a spacious interior featuring comfortable seating for passengers. Its spacious interior provides abundant seating capacity, and each seat is equipped with a unique storage system. By opening upwards, the seats offer convenient storage compartments for passengers to safely store their belongings during the ride. The bus is covered in glass, creating a futuristic design while allowing for panoramic views of the surroundings.
